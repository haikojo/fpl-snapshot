About this app

An unofficial Fantasy Premier League snapshot for quick check-ins between gameweeks. Uses publicly available FPL data to show deadlines and performance trends. Read-only and not affiliated with the Premier League or FPL.

HOW TO READ THIS

  • Green ▲ means your rank improved.
  • Blue rank line: lower is better.
  • Form5 = points across the last 5 gameweeks.

DATA NOTES

  • Uses public FPL endpoints (read-only).
  • No login, no tracking.
  • Cached for speed.
